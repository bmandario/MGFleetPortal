Who We Are

About MG Fleet Management System

A world-class fleet management company built on the Philippines' most trusted automotive service network.

MG Fleet Management System (MG FMS) is the fleet management arm of Master Garage Philippines — the country's leading multi-branch automotive service chain.

We are not just a software platform. We are a complete fleet management company — combining digital technology, dedicated coordination, roadworthy assessment, and a nationwide physical service network into one seamless subscription.

While global companies like Element Fleet, Holman, and FleetNet have built billion-dollar businesses doing exactly this, MG FMS brings the same world-class model to Philippine fleet clients — with local expertise, local branches, and local accountability.

Our Mission

To eliminate the hidden cost of unmanaged fleets — by giving every fleet client in the Philippines the tools, service network, and expertise to protect their vehicles and maximize asset value.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in Philippine fleet management — the company every serious fleet operator calls first.

Our Commitment

Every enrolled vehicle will be treated as if it belongs to us. Your fleet's performance is our reputation.
