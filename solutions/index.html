How It Works

A simple four-step process from enrollment to ongoing management.

1

Fleet Enrollment

We onboard your vehicles into the MG FMS system. Each vehicle is registered, baseline-assessed, and assigned to your nearest MG branch.

2

Roadworthy Assessment

Every enrolled vehicle undergoes a comprehensive roadworthy assessment. We document the current condition and flag any immediate concerns.

3

Scheduled Maintenance Management

Your fleet coordinator manages all PMS schedules. Services are pre-booked at your designated MG branch with zero effort from your team.

4

Ongoing Monitoring & Reporting

We track every service, every cost, every record. You receive monthly fleet health reports and have access to your complete fleet data anytime.
